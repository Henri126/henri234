About the Album

"Face Tat"
is the debut studio album by American drummer. and multi-instrumentalist
Zach Hill.
The album was released on October 19, 2010,
and features a blend of experimental rock,
noise, and electronic elements.
It showcases Hill's unique and eclectic musical style.
